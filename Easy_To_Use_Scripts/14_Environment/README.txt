14_Environment (Unity 2022 LTS)
------------------------------
Environment mechanics that show up often.

Scripts:
- HazardDamage2D: spikes/lava trigger damage
- FallingPlatform2D: platform drops after stepping on it
- BreakableObject2D: takes damage and breaks (uses Health)
- OneWayPlatform2D: simple one-way platform (platform effector)

Notes:
- OneWayPlatform2D expects a PlatformEffector2D on the object.
